Alan Turing at age 16 circa 1928.

Some of Turing's achievements:

  • 1935 - Proved the Central Limit Theorum
  • 1936 - First theorized what we now know as "Touring Machines"
  • 1938 - Lead codebreaking effort against Germany in WWII
  • 1940 - Developed the Bombe to decrypt German Enigma cipher
  • 1946 - Appointed an Officer of the Order of the British Empire by King George VI
  • 1947 - Sabbatical year in Cambridge where he wrote about Intelligent Machinery
  • 1950 - Developed the Turing Test and addressed the problem of Artificial Intelligence
